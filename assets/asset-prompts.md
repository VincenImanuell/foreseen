# Foreseen — asset generation prompts

Central home for **all** image/asset generation prompts (Gemini, etc.). Want a new
asset? Add its prompt here under a new `##` section, then drop the generated file in
this `assets/` folder.

## Shared style guide (apply to every asset)
- **Positioning:** Foreseen is a competitive **mind sport**; Rock-Paper-Scissors is just
  the costume. Assets must NOT lean on hands / fists / rock-paper-scissors gestures.
- **Theme:** dark oracle + cyberpunk terminal. Pure black background.
- **Palette:** gold = rank / achievement / inner glow · electric cyan-teal = data streams ·
  deep purple = mystical smoke aura · white text with subtle gold shimmer.
- **Motif:** the all-seeing oracle eye with a hexagon pupil (blockchain).
- Generated files live in `assets/`; raw link pattern:
  `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/VincenImanuell/foreseen/main/assets/<file>`

## 1. App logo → `assets/logo_foreseen.jpg` ✅ generated

```
A minimalist emblem logo for a blockchain game called "FORESEEN", centered on a
pure black background. The logo is a single stylized all-seeing oracle eye,
almond-shaped and perfectly symmetrical.

- Pupil: a small, sharp HEXAGON glowing from within — a nod to blockchain /
  on-chain technology.
- Iris: concentric rings of fine, luminous GOLD filaments radiating outward from
  the hexagon pupil like a scrying lens or radar scan — evoking foresight, data,
  and prediction. Clean, geometric, hypnotic. NOT a literal human iris.
- Layered glow around the eye:
  • Inner: warm gold gradient glowing from within
  • Mid: electric cyan/teal streaks extending outward like data streams
  • Outer: deep purple aura, wide and diffused like smoke

Below the eye, the word "FORESEEN" in a clean geometric sans-serif, all caps,
wide letter spacing, white with a subtle gold shimmer.

Pure black background only. Square 1:1 composition, eye centered. Mood: mystical
oracle meets cyberpunk terminal — serious, premium, cinematic. Sharp vector-like
edges with a subtle glow bloom, high detail.

Do NOT include any hands, fingers, fists, palms, or rock-paper-scissors gestures.
The iris must be abstract light and data, never hand shapes.
```

**Variations**
- **Icon only:** delete the "Below the eye, the word FORESEEN..." paragraph (avatar/favicon).
- **Portrait/tarot:** replace "Square 1:1 composition" with "Tall vertical portrait format, tarot-card proportions".
- **Alt iris:** replace the iris paragraph with "a glowing clock-like ring of thin radial tick marks" (evokes seeing the future / time).
