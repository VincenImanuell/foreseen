# Bot honesty

Foreseen supports player-deployed agents and cold-start opponent bots, but fake
usage is not acceptable.

Allowed:

- Joining real player-created matches.
- Testing strategies on Celo Sepolia.
- Labeling bot wallets clearly.

Not allowed:

- Bot-vs-bot funded loops.
- Fake DAU, fake volume, or fake download automation.
- Reporting bot traffic as organic players.
