# CELO Campaign Readiness

Keep public activity useful for reviewers and safe for the live Foreseen game.

Checklist:

- Keep campaign commits tied to player-facing Celo or MiniPay work.
- Prefer documentation, frontend copy, SDK examples, and QA notes over contract edits.
- Do not split one trivial change into many commits only to inflate activity.
- Confirm the repository remains public and build artifacts stay out of Git.
- Leave mainnet contract addresses and deployment records unchanged unless a real release requires it.
